Lumio English Writing-Unit Two Lecture

Lumio English Level II - English Writing Unit 2（Family）

Course Length：1/2/2021 - 2021（Continues）

This Unit is currently under progress, so the study materials will be updated continuously…

Unit Summary：This unit topic is Family, the lectures mainly focus on the common grammar and sentence structures student use while introducing their family members, which include:
  1. The plural and singular forms for third person pronouns (he/she/they).
  2. Understand all the common verbs covered in this unit.
  3. Understand the grammer rules for third person present indefinite tense.
  4. Understand both the singular and plural forms of the third person possessive adjectives (his/her/their).
  5. Be very familiar with the unit vocabulary (family & common verbs).

1. Lesson 1
 - Lesson Summary：Introduce the Unit vocabulary - family members, introduce the grammar rules for third person pronouns (he&she) and related sentence structures.

 - Lesson Summary：Review vocabulary from last lesson, review gammar rules for third person pronouns (he&she), introduce three new family vocabulary, introduce the grammar rules for third person plural pronoun - they and related sentence structures.

 - Lesson Summary：Review vocabulary from last lesson, review gammar rules for third person pronouns (he/she/they),introduce two new family vocabulary, introduce the grammer rules for third person present indefinite tense and related sentence structures.
