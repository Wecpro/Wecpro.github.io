Lumio English Speaking-Unit Two Lecture

Lumio English Level II - Speaking Unit 2（Family）

Course Length：12/19/2021 - 2021（Continues）

This Unit is currently under progress, so the study materials will be updated continuously…

Unit Summary：This unit is about family and will be focusing on vocabulary and dialogues related to intoducing family members, which includes：

  1. Learn family member vocabulary and how to introduce one’s family member.
  2. Learn job Vocabulary and how to describe the jobs of family members.
  3. Learn adjectives that describe people’s appreance.(EX: tall, short,pretty,etc.)
  4. Combine all the vocabulary and sentence structure learned to fluently introduce one’s family memebrs, including their appearance and job.

Click to Download Powerpoint:

1. Lesson 1
 - Lesson Summary：Learn 8 family vocabulary, learn how to introduce family members, understand how to pronounce words with the phonetic alphabet æ.
2. Lesson 2
 - Lesson Summary：Learn 6 new family vocabulary, learn to sing the Christmas song.
3. Lesson 3
 - Lesson Summary：Learn 6 new job vocabulary, learn how to ask/describe one’s job.
4. Lesson 4
 - Lesson Summary：Learn 5 new job vocabulary, learn how to ask/describe one’s job.
5. Lesson 5
 - Lesson Summary：Learn the 10 adjectives that describe people’s appreance and combine them with the job vocabulary to describe family members.
