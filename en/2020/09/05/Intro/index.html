Lumio EDU Mission Statement

This is the English site of Lumio Education, and it is for demo purposes only at this time. The main target students of Lumio EDU right now are Chinese students in the poverty regions of China, so all English learning materials post at this site are in Chinese. However, we are open to new ideas, advices and cooperations, if you would like to be part of our team or wanted to get connected, please feel free to reach out to us!

Founder’s words-Why lumio

As the founder and president of iHelp, a student organization at my school that sends volunteers to remote parts of China to teach English, I have taught English in secluded, rural areas of China and have hosted charity bazaars to raise money for underfunded schools and poor families within these areas. My eagerness to curtail educational disparities between rural and urban China has also inspired me to launch Lumio, an online teaching platform that provides free English courses taught by volunteer teachers like myself. Thus, even students in the most secluded areas of China can access quality English instruction through their computer or mobile device.
