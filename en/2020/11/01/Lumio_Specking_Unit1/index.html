Lumio English Speaking-Unit One Lecture

Lumio English Level II - English Speaking Unit 1（Self Introduction）

Course Length：10/31/2020 - 12/13/2020

Unit Summary：This unit is about self-introduction and will be focusing on vocabulary and dialogues related to introducing oneself, which includes：

  1. Learn how to use the word “favorite”.
  2. Learn the English numbers (1-12) and how to ask or answer questions related to numbers.
  3. Learn how to ask questions related to one’s age or the amount of objects one have and answer them.
  4. Learn Vocabulary about colors and how to ask or answer one’s favorite color and an object’s color
  5. Learn Vocabulary about clothes and how to ask or answer what one is wearing.
  6. Learn the three vowel phonetics æ，e，I，and the definition of vowels and consonants.
